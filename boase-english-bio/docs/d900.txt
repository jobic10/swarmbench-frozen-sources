HUGGINS, WILLIAM (_brother of the preceding_). _b._ Liverpool 1820; member of Liverpool Academy; painter at Chester 1861 to death; his horses, cattle and poultry pictures were his best work; exhibited 31 pictures at R.A., 8 at B.I. and 1 at Suffolk st. 1842–75; exhibited many pictures at Liverpool, Manchester, Dublin, Edinburgh and Glasgow. _d._ Christleton near Chester 25 Feb. 1884. _Biograph, Feb. 1882 pp._ 217–23; _Liverpool Mercury 28 Feb. 1884 p._ 6. NOTE.—His sister Sarah Huggins, a painter of flowers and fruit and then of architectural views in Chester and Liverpool, _d._ Liverpool, May 1869. _The Builder 8 May 1869 p._ 369.
