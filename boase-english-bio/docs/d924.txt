HUNTER, REV. JOHN (_youngest son of Rev. Andrew Hunter, minister of Tron ch. Edin._, _d._ 1809). _b._ Edin. 1788; presbyterian minister of Swinton, Berwickshire 1814–32; assistant minister of Tron ch. Edin. after a contest with the kirk session which was decided in house of lords Oct. 1832, minister of Tron ch. to death; D.D. of univ. of Edin. 29 May 1847. _d._ 9 Regent ter. Edinburgh 21 June 1866. _Crombie’s Modern Athenians_ (1882) 27–8, _portrait_; _Scott’s Fasti, i part i, p._ 61.
