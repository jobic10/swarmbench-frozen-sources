BROOM, HERBERT (_only son of Herbert Broom of Kidderminster_). _b._ Kidderminster 1815; ed. at Trin. coll. Cam., 40 wrangler 1837, B.A. 1837, M.A. 1854, LLD. 1864; barrister I.T. 20 Nov. 1840; professor of common law to council of legal education Jany. 1873 to 1875; author of _Practical rules for determining parties to actions_ 1843, _2 ed._ 1846; _A selection of legal maxims_ 1845, _6 ed._ 1884; _Commentaries on the common law_ 1856, _7 ed._ 1884; _Constitutional law_ 1866, _2 ed._ 1885, and of 2 novels _The missing will 3 vols._ 1877 and _The unjust steward 2 vols._ 1879. _d._ The Priory, Orpington, Kent 2 May 1882.
