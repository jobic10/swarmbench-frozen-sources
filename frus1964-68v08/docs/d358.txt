358. Telegram From the Department of State to the Mission of the European Office of the United Nations
Date: 1967-05-15
Source: Department of State, Eyes Only Reels.

194633. Personal from the President for Ambassador Roth. Francis has just reported to me that we have a Kennedy Round bargain. I want you to know what a first-rate job you, Mike and your team have done, and how much I appreciate it. Congratulations. I look forward to your report.
