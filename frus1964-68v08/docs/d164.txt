164. Message From President Johnson to the President's Special Assistant (Califano)
Date: 1967-12-22
Source: Johnson Library, National Security File, Subject File, Balance of Payments, Vol.

Point two of the Ackley memo which begins "through the first three quarters of 1967, the deficit widened,"-which you transmitted and I received Saturday, December 23, 0230 -plus the first paragraph only of third point that follows, plus all the points in Paragraph Four should be outlined by you or Fowler to Mills over the telephone-repeat, on the telephone, not in wire-and let him chew on this for the next day or two before I return. Every effort should be made to get Fowler, Ackley and company in complete agreement. This will be difficult, but you can handle tough assignments. See you Sunday.
